Why donate blood?
A single donation can save up to three lives. Regular donations help maintain blood availability for surgeries, accidents, and emergencies.
Basic Eligibility
Age 18–60, good health, Hb ≥ 12.5 g/dL.
Donation Interval
At least 90 days between donations.
Stay Hydrated
Drink water before and after donation.
Safe Process
Sterile equipment is used for every donor.
